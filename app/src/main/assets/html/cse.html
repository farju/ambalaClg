Computer Science and Engineering

Dr. Surender Singh

Associate Professor & Head, Deptt. of Computer Science & Engineering

On the behalf of students & staff members of Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), I welcome you all. This department provides you a wonderful opportunity to learn state-of-the-art technology. It grooms its student to face the challenging world by strengthening their technical and inter-personal skills by providing focused training in academic as well as in non-academic fields.

Besides all the academic work, you get the chance of participating in extra-curricular activities, participating in programming contests and organization of technical festivals, seminars and so on.


Software development, IT-based services, universities, telecom operators and vendors ' name any of the job sectors and you will find a stronghold of CSE graduates in each of them.

The graduates of this department have proved their ability to deliver the best in corporate environment and also take the performance standard to a higher level.
They have fetched the recognition of renowned companies by their sheer hard work and ingenuity. Many of our graduates and postgraduates are working as faculty members in various reputed public and private universities. With the continued effort CSE is committed to deliver the best education and prepare students to compete with the global challenges to progress into a better future. I wish you a happy learning and welcome you to be a part of this journey.
